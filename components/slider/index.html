--- title: Slider layout: component ---

One of many graphical user interface components that users have had experience with. One of many experienced examples can be changing the volume setting on the computer or on a video streaming website.

A slider is control that lets the user choose from a predetermined range of values.

by Ernesto Garcia

Typical Appearance
